Nutritional Benefits of Nigerian Foods

Beans

High in protein and fiber, helps muscle growth and digestion.

Yam

Provides energy-rich carbohydrates for daily activities.

Fish

Rich in omega-3 fatty acids for brain and heart health.

Vegetables

Boost immunity and provide essential vitamins.

Healthy Eating Tips

  • Eat a balanced diet with protein, carbs, and vegetables.
  • Reduce excessive oil in cooking.
  • Drink enough water daily.
  • Include fresh fruits regularly.
  • Avoid excessive processed foods.
